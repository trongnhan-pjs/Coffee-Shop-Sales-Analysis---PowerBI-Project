# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis---PowerBI-Project

**1. Overview**

The Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze and monitor sales performance across multiple dimensions, including time, product categories, and store locations. The dashboard delivers clear, data-driven insights to support business decision-making in a retail coffee shop environment.

**2. Purpose**

This project aims to:

- Monitor key sales KPIs (revenue, orders, quantity sold)
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns across time (daily & hourly)
- Identify top-performing products and store locations
- Support operational and strategic decisions with real-time insights

**3. Tech Stack**

- Power BI (Dashboard Development & Data Visualization)
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation - ETL)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculated measures and KPIs
- Data Modeling (relationships, star schema design)

**4. Data Source**

Internal transactional dataset containing approximately 14,000+ sales records from a coffee shop environment. The dataset includes detailed information on order dates, sales revenue, quantities sold, product categories (e.g., coffee, tea, bakery items), and store locations, enabling comprehensive time-series and performance analysis.

**6. Key Insights**

- Revenue is highest between 8 AM – 11 AM, reflecting strong demand for coffee before working hours.
- Sales remain consistent from Tuesday to Friday, indicating steady demand during weekdays.
- Sales volume tends to slightly decrease on weekends compared to weekdays.
- The Coffee category contributes the largest share of total revenue, especially Espresso and Brewed Coffee.
- Locations such as Hell’s Kitchen and Astoria generate a significant portion of total sales.
- Daily revenue remains relatively stable, showing no extreme fluctuations → indicating stable business performance.
- A small group of top-performing products contributes a large share of revenue → ideal for focused marketing and upselling strategies.
